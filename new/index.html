What does "Commerce' mean?

According to Merriam-Webster, commerce is defined as:

(1) social intercourse: interchange of ideas, opinions, or sentiments, (2) the exchange or buying and selling of commodities on a large scale involving transportation from place to place.

We will take this standard definition as the starting scope, and hold to a broadly interpreted view of what commerce is and can be.
