The FutureCommerce Hackathon focus is understanding and building next generation products, transactions, experiences, relationships and business models. With the partnership of Massachuetts Legal Hackers and the ComputableLaw research team of MIT Media Lab's Human Dynamics group, a strong set of legal resources, talent and innovative new solutions will be made available for participants to leverage as part of addressing key challenges to innovation and entreprenuership in the new economy.

This is a hybrid online and in-person event, offering a syposium for participants to engage with experts, top entreprenuers and thought leaders, a series of skill building, learning and social activities and featuring a full MIT hackathon. This hackathon will use the MIT PrototypeJam style, providing challenges and resources for team-based rapid design and prototyping projects, culminating in a round of competitive judging.
